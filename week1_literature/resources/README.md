# Resources

The following papers are required for Week 1. Obtain them through your institution's journal access.

## Anchor papers

- **Anchor 1 (vocabulary):** Cuyckens, F.; Claeys, M. Mass spectrometry in the structural analysis of flavonoids. *J. Mass Spectrom.* 2004, 39, 1–15. https://doi.org/10.1002/jms.585
- **Anchor 2 (mechanistic):** Ma, Y.L. et al. Characterization of flavone and flavonol aglycones by collision-induced dissociation. *Rapid Commun. Mass Spectrom.* 1997, 11, 1357–1364.
                              Fabre, N. et al. Determination of flavone, flavonol, and flavanone aglycones by negative ion LC-ESI-MS/MS. *J. Am. Soc. Mass Spectrom.* 2001, 12, 707–715.
## Background reading

- De Vijlder, T. et al. A tutorial in small molecule identification via electrospray ionization mass spectrometry. *Mass Spectrom. Rev.* 2018, 37, 607–629. https://doi.org/10.1002/mas.21548
